Guacamole Cheeseburger

Description

A Cheeseburger with a Southwestern citrus twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados - halved, peeled, and pitted
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 1 fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
  • ½ cup diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup minced onion
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon salt to taste
